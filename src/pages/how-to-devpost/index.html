What is Devpost?

Devpost is the platform that pretty much all hackathons use for project submission. This is where you can tell us all about your project and be eligible to have your project judged and potentially win prizes. Teams will submit to Devpost AND present their project to judges in-person. Click the “Go to Devpost” button at the top of the page to visit our Devpost page and start your project submission! You can edit your submission even after your press submit.

Team

Add all the people who worked with you on the project. You can invite them by email or give them an invite link.

Project Info

Add the name of your project and a short description of it. Remember the name you chose because this is the name we will use for your table assignment.

Project Story and Image Gallery

This is the best part! Go into as much detail as you would like and tell us all about your project. Talk about all the features your project has and how it was made. Tell us what motivated you to make it, the things you learned while making it, and any obstacles you encountered. Your Devpost plays a big role in how your project is judged so we recommend putting lots of thought and effort into it! And don’t forget to add lots of images and videos of your project in action!

Github Repo and Links

You are REQUIRED to submit a GitHub repository containing your code. We want to see your development process and what’s behind all the amazing things in your project! Any of our organizers can help you with uploading your code to GitHub if you need help. Furthermore, if you deployed your project publicly on the web or have any useful links for your project, we’d love to see them in this section.

Video Demo

A brief video demo showing your project in action is REQUIRED for all submissions. Upload your video to YouTube and add a link to it here. Make sure your video is publicly accessible (public or unlisted) and is no more than 3 minutes long.

Tracks

This is the most important part of the entire submission. Select the tracks that you want to be judged for. If you do not select your desired tracks, you will likely NOT be judged for those tracks and or be eligible for a prize in that category. You may submit to multiple tracks. All projects are automatically entered into Best Overall.

Submission

Finally, submit your project and you’re good to go! You can edit your submission even after your press submit.
