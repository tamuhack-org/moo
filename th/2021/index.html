About TAMUhack

At TAMUhack, teams spend the weekend working on innovative software and hardware solutions to real-world problems in a community full of mentorship.

We'll provide you with all the resources and mentors you need to work on something cool. You'll have the freedom to create a product, learn new skills or just have fun working on a project with friends. We'll also provide free discord workspaces and free swag—just be sure to bring plenty of resumes for all of the awesome sponsors and mentors you'll meet! Check them out below!

Due to concerns about spreading of COVID-19, this year’s TAMUhack will be hosted online, but that does not mean it will be any less exciting! The hackathon will be hosted on Hopin with lots of workshops, events, and interactive competitions to help create the engaging atmosphere that comes with in-person hackathons.
