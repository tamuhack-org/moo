What is TAMUhack?

TAMUhack is a 24 hour event where teams create innovative software and hardware solutions to real-world problems in a community of mentorship.


You'll have the resources, mentors, and freedom to create a unique product, learn new skills or just have fun collaborating with friends. We'll also provide all the free food and free swag—just be sure to bring your resume for all of the awesome sponsors and mentors you'll meet! Check them out below!

Who can attend?

All students can sign up to be a hacker. Regardless of your experience, education, or background, as long as you are excited about learning, building, and having fun, we’d love for you to attend. If you are currently under the age of 18, please contact our team at hello@tamuhack.com to fill out a waiver form.

When is the deadline to apply for TAMUhack?

The deadline for registration is January 22, at 23:59. We'll be doing rolling admissions until that date. Our capacity is limited, so make sure to apply early!
