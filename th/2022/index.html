About TAMUhack

At TAMUhack, teams spend the weekend working on innovative software and hardware solutions to real-world problems in a community full of mentorship.

We'll provide you with all the resources and mentors you need to work on something cool. You'll have the freedom to create a product, learn new skills or just have fun working on a project with friends. We'll also provide all the free food and free swag—just be sure to bring plenty of resumes for all of the awesome sponsors and mentors you'll meet! Virtual hackers will only receive swag if they win.

Check them out below!

COVID-19 Guidelines

We’re doing everything we can to make TAMUhack as safe as possible for attendees and we need your help to do so. In-person attendees must satisfy one of the following criteria - a negative test* taken on or after January 15th, or proof of vaccination (presented digitally or physically). Failure to satisfy either of these requirements will deny entrance even if an acceptance has been received. Hackers are encouraged to participate virtually if they will be unable to fulfill the preconditions.

*Test must be a negative PCR, antibody, or Curative test (available around the Texas A&M campus and other universities) taken on or after January 15th, 2022. At home tests DO NOT count.
